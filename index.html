works of Art

Glass Elephant Pro

Ground-up build, brand development

Worked directly with Glass Elephant's owner developing a fully-functional site from wireframes under a tight deadline. Wrote a vast majority of copy for the site, and optimized images considerably.

Making the site user-friendly for a wide demographic was of particular importance to Glass Elephant. Several design elements and branding options were tested with the client before the current site iteration was finalized.

  • Highlights:
  • Complete brand overhaul; heavy image authoring and editing work
  • Crafted fast, lightweight JavaScript solution for FAQ and Equipment page navigation
  • Assisted with site migration and hosting solutions
  • Visit glasselephantpro.com

Jazz In Austin

Design Consultation / Debugging

Worked with lead developer Aaron Allen to update JazzInAustin's somewhat outdated visual design. Also assisted in testing and debugging the site's custom-built PHP/MySQL login system.

  • Highlights:
  • Revamped outdated stylesheet with modern design elements
  • Assisted lead developer in login system testing and debugging
  • Visit jazzinaustin.com

Candy DeClue Music

Ground-Up build

Full-stack site build, including custom login system and scratch-built calendar management system, as well as site-wide copywriting and image editing.

Creating a striking brand for Ms. DeClue was of particular importance. Many site visitors are either potential clients or audience members who had seen a live show. The final site design and color scheme was chosen from several performance photographs and wardrobe selections.

  • Highlights:
  • Custom login system in PHP/MySQL
  • Scratch-built calendar management system in JavaScript/PHP/MySQL
  • Visit candydecluemusic.com

about Art

I'm a web developer currently living in Austin, producing sites and customer-facing web solutions for clients on a freelance basis. I also occasionally work as a web design consultant.

I'm a fanatic about code and design--one of those that can stay up through the wee hours getting things pixel-perfect.

When I'm not digging through web code, you're likely to find me behind either a game controller or a guitar.
